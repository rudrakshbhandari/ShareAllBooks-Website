About ShareAllBooks

ShareAllBooks connects you to nearby readers through a user-friendly platform for donating, selling, and buying books, fostering knowledge, environmental sustainability, and community.

Welcome to ShareAllBooks, a revolutionary platform designed to bridge the gap between readers, foster community connections, and make education more accessible across the United Arab Emirates. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a space where books can find new homes, knowledge can be freely exchanged, and individuals can come together to build a brighter future. At ShareAllBooks, we believe that books hold the key to unlocking endless possibilities. Our platform goes beyond being just a marketplace for buying and selling books – it's a hub where stories are shared, friendships are formed, and a love for learning is nurtured. Whether you're a student seeking textbooks, an avid reader in search of the next great novel, or someone with books longing for a purpose, this is the place for you.






Features

Browse Ads

Browse through ads by searching for a book, or filter by genre or emirate

Post Ads

Post an ad of a book in just 30 seconds

Meet fellow readers

Direct contact between buyers and sellers to foster community relations

Find nearby ads

Filter ads by searching for books in your area or by students in your school or college

Track History

Manage the books you have donated or sold, with the option to relist or delete any ad

Editable Profile

Edit your profile - name, phone number, location, school or college anytime
